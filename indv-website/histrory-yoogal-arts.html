Histroy of Yoogal Arts

Yoogal arts was officially founded in the year 1985 by Sudershan Pokhrel. Having participated in several businesses arts and advertisement, now he aims to exhibit his arts also in your schools, colleges with fascinating decoration needed to attract your students and preschool children. He also displayed his extra ordinary talents while he lived in India for a couple years and he was awarded as a second best artist in Northern India in the year 2005.

Work and Accomplishment

In his long career in this field he has worked with many big companies in a national level. He has helped many companies grow their business though his artistic advertisements. He had worked earlier with different big and small business such as Wai Wai, cosmetic products, Tobourg. Also he had completed multiple school project such as Lycium Boarding school, ABC boarding school, BHaktapur academic school. Also he had worked with bank such as Laxmi bank. In the year 1995, he also completed a project in Tribhuban International Airport working on different Aero plane paintings and projects. His work has gone from those kind of big companies into small projects such as Grocery stores, super markets, jewelry store, hotels, restaurants and so on.
