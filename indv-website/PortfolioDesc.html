Portfolio Description

There is much to see, say, and write about a painting. A piece of art is completed by composition, the colors, the vibrancy, the state of mind of the artist. All of my paintings are not only formed by my skills but also much pondering and passion to do what I imagine. My paintings represent all kinds of non-verbal communication that every children use. Every piece of art consists of a clildren language that sometimes we forget or ignore to speak, but my art speaks them all.
