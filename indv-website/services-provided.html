Hoarding Board

A hoarding is a very large board at the side of a road or on the side of a building, which is used for displaying advertisements and posters. It is very good way of advertising your services and products to wide range of people. It is generally little costlier then normal sign boards and banners, becasue it requires lots of man power and time for making.
